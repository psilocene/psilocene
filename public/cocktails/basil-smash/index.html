basil smash

  • 10 basil leaves
  • 1⁄2 lemon
  • 20ml simple syrup
  • 50ml Plymouth gin
  1. Extract juice from lemon into shaker
  2. Add basil leaves, then lemon to shaker and muddle well
  3. Add simple syrup and mix
  4. Fill with ice, top with gin
  5. Strain, into glass with or without ice
  6. Garnish with basil leaf
